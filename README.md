# Undrone

A puzzle game where you control a mutually coupled swarm of drones to solve complex tasks, featuring time as a controllable, visible dimension. Built with **Godot Engine** and **F#**.

## Concept

In **Undrone**, you don't just guide a swarm through space; you orchestrate their trajectories across both space and time. 

### Key Features
- **Mutually Coupled Swarm**: The drones are not independent. Their behaviors, physical states, or control inputs are coupled. Actions taken by one drone might influence, restrict, or boost the capabilities of others in the swarm.
- **Temporal Dimensions**: Time is a visible, tactile axis. You can preview, scrub, and manipulate timelines. Solve spatial puzzles by scheduling drone actions in the past, present, or future, and witness the ripple effects across coupled systems.
- **Physics-Based Puzzles**: Navigate hazards, activate switches, and coordinate precise movements where momentum and coupling dynamics matter.

## Tech Stack

- **Game Engine**: [Godot Engine](https://godotengine.org/) (4.x with .NET Support)
- **Language**: [F#](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/fsharp/) (.NET Core)
